Elzanaty et al.
2020 — Base paper		 ACM + PCS for FSO / IM-DD channels Gap
Uses unipolar PAM only — no coherent QAM; no multi-objective scoring; no hysteresis switching; no spectral efficiency or power co-optimisation. Fixed shaping parameter with no switching logic. 		Addressed
Extends to coherent 16-QAM with MB-PCS. Adds three-mode ACM switching (QPSK / 16-QAM / PCS-16QAM). Introduces composite score jointly optimising BER (w1=0.5), SE (w2=0.3), power (w3=0.2) with ±2 dB hysteresis dead-band.
Cho & Winzer
2019/cited 2020+		 PCS theory & rate adaptability for optical fiber Gap
Reviews PCS in isolation — no adaptive switching between formats, no BER/power multi-objective framework, no hysteresis control, no EON context. 		Addressed
Applies PCS as a third ACM mode within a live EON OSNR feedback loop. Hysteresis prevents format oscillation. Shaping gain (+1.20 dB) is verified within the composite scoring system.
Oliveira et al.
2021		 PCS for amplifier-less coherent links Gap
Fixed single modulation format with PCS — no format switching, no BER/SE/power joint objective, no hysteresis, no EON architecture. 		Addressed
Multi-format switching with PCS-16QAM as a selectable mode. Power reduction (1.3 W vs 1.5 W, −13%) is one of three co-optimised objectives, not a standalone goal.
Reihani et al.
2022		 ANN-based adaptive modulation in EON Gap
Optimises spectral efficiency only using ML — no PCS, no power objective, no BER compliance guarantee, no hysteresis, no closed-form analytical model. 		Addressed
Deterministic closed-form ACM with PCS — no ML training overhead. BER compliance (10⁻⁶) is the highest-priority objective (w1=0.5). Full 10–30 dB OSNR BER compliance is verified analytically.
Klinkowski & Jaworski
2022		 OSNR-aware lightpath planning in MCF-EON Gap
Static lightpath planning — no real-time adaptive modulation switching, no PCS, no multi-objective scoring, no hysteresis. 		Addressed
Real-time OSNR feedback drives dynamic format switching. ±2 dB dead-band stabilises the finite-state machine. PCS adds a third adaptive degree of freedom beyond QPSK/16-QAM.
Karandin et al.
2023		 Probabilistic ML-based QoT & margin reduction in EON Gap
Focuses only on margin management via ML — no PCS, no energy efficiency objective, no hysteresis, no composite multi-objective score. 		Addressed
Uses a deterministic 2 dB uncertainty buffer (∆m) instead of ML-trained margins, combined with PCS shaping gain (+1.20 dB) to achieve BER-compliant, power-efficient operation without training data.
Liu et al. (FBD-PCS)
2023		 Feedback-driven adaptive PCS for fiber-THz systems Gap
Adaptive shaping parameter selection per channel — but single modulation format, no QPSK/16-QAM mode switching, no spectral efficiency or power co-objective, no EON targeting. 		Addressed
Fixed λ=0.35 at the entropy-gain knee balances SE and BER without per-symbol feedback complexity. Three-mode format switching achieves 86% SE gain over QPSK at full BER compliance.
Cheng et al.
2024		 ML-based RMSA with modulation-adaptive routing in EON Gap
Network-level routing and spectrum allocation — no PCS, no power objective, no hysteresis, no closed-form BER model, no per-link real-time format switching. 		Addressed
Per-link real-time ACM with PCS-16QAM. Closed-form BER expressions (no ML inference delay). Hysteresis prevents switching oscillation at the link level, complementing network-layer RMSA solutions.
Liu et al. (4D PCS)
2023		 PCS over multi-dimensional constellations for long-haul Gap
Fixed 4D modulation with PCS — no adaptive switching, no power/SE co-objective, no hysteresis, high implementation complexity not suitable for real-time EON transponders. 		Addressed
Uses 2D PAM-4-based PCS-16QAM (lower complexity) with a practical normalised composite score for real-time mode selection — directly implementable in standard coherent transponders.
Liang & Kahn
2025		 Optimal vs MB shaping distributions for optical channels Gap
Theoretical distribution analysis only — no ACM framework, no switching algorithm, no BER/SE/power joint optimisation, no EON simulation. 		Addressed
