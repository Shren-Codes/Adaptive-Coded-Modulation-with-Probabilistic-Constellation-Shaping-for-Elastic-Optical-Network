Your day — Chennai, 8 PM, IPL match night on JioFiber
Network congestion during peak hours (7 PM to 11 PM) reduces speeds by 20–30% on Indian ISP fiber links. India ranked 87th out of 181 countries in median fixed broadband speed (58.62 Mbps download) as of late 2023.
2:00 AM — Off-peak
Jio backbone: near-empty, high OSNR (~26 dB)
Jio's optical backbone between Chennai and Mumbai data centre is carrying almost no traffic. Amplifiers are cool, fiber is clean. OSNR is high. Your Netflix downloads at 150 Mbps, no issues.
Current fixed system: locked to 16-QAM. Works fine but wastes 13% extra power all night long.
Your ACM+PCS: automatically selects PCS-16QAM — same throughput, 1.3 W instead of 1.5 W, +1.20 dB shaping gain.
8:00 AM — Morning ramp-up
Offices open, OSNR starts dropping (~21 dB)
Millions of employees log into Microsoft Teams, upload to Google Drive, push code to GitHub. OSNR dips to ~21 dB — right inside the ±2 dB hysteresis dead-band (18–22 dB).
Current fixed system: still using 16-QAM, BER starts rising quietly — most users won't notice yet.
Your ACM+PCS: hysteresis holds PCS-16QAM. No unnecessary switching. System stays stable.
8:00 PM — IPL match + peak hours
300 million Indians streaming simultaneously, OSNR crashes (~14 dB)
Peak hours occur between 7 PM and 11 PM — streaming, gaming, and video calls cause network congestion, leading to buffering and slowdowns. Jio's backbone OSNR on the Chennai–Mumbai route drops hard.
Current fixed system: still locked to 16-QAM. BER explodes above 10⁻³. Jio's SDN controller panics, drops your connection to 480p. You see the dreaded buffer wheel mid-over.
Your ACM+PCS: detects OSNR < 18 dB. Hysteresis crossed. Automatically switches to QPSK in under 1 ms. BER drops back to 10⁻⁸. You keep watching at HD — zero buffering.
10:30 PM — Match ends, crowd leaves
Traffic drains, OSNR recovers (~23 dB)
Millions of users go to sleep. OSNR climbs back above 22 dB — past the upper hysteresis boundary.
Current fixed system: operator manually reviews, may or may not upgrade format until the next maintenance window.
Your ACM+PCS: composite score re-evaluates. PCS-16QAM wins. System auto-upgrades. Your late-night YouTube at 4K just works.
